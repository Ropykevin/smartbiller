About SmartBiller

Revolutionizing property management in Kenya with innovative technology and exceptional service.

Our Mission

To empower Kenyan landlords with modern, efficient, and user-friendly property management tools that streamline rent collection, tenant communication, and financial tracking. We believe every landlord deserves professional-grade software that makes property management effortless.

Our Vision

To become the leading property management platform in East Africa, setting new standards for digital transformation in real estate. We envision a future where property management is seamless, transparent, and profitable for all stakeholders.

Our Story

How It All Started

SmartBiller was born from a simple observation: Kenyan landlords were struggling with manual, time-consuming property management processes. Traditional methods were prone to errors, lacked transparency, and made it difficult to track payments and communicate with tenants effectively.

Our founders, experienced in both technology and real estate, recognized the opportunity to create a solution that would address these pain points. They envisioned a platform that would combine the power of modern technology with deep understanding of local market needs.

Today, SmartBiller serves hundreds of landlords across Kenya, helping them manage their properties more efficiently and profitably. We continue to innovate and expand our features based on user feedback and market demands.
