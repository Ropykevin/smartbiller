Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and are prorated.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees. You only pay the monthly subscription fee after your free trial ends.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all plans. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and major credit cards for payment. All payments are processed securely.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. No long-term contracts required. You'll have access until the end of your billing period.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is safe. You can export all your data before canceling, and we keep it for 30 days in case you want to reactivate your account.
